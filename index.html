What this dashboard answers

  • Top-performing teams by wins and win percentage
  • High-scoring venues and chase-friendly grounds
  • Innings-wise scoring patterns (1st vs 2nd innings)
  • Toss decision impact (bat vs field)
  • Success patterns for chasing and defending 300+ totals
